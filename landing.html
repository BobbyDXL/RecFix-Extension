RecFix

Fix and fine-tune your YouTube recommendations

About RecFix

RecFix is a Chrome extension that helps you take control of your YouTube recommendations. Select videos you like, and RecFix will create a curated playlist of similar content, helping you discover videos that match your interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my playlist being created in the wrong YouTube account?

This happens when you have multiple Google accounts in Chrome. There are two ways to fix this:

Method 1: Make your preferred account primary (Recommended)
  1. Sign out of all Google accounts in Chrome
  2. Sign in with the YouTube account you want to use with RecFix first
  3. Then sign in to your other accounts
  4. The first account you sign in becomes the primary account for Chrome extensions
Method 2: Temporary account removal
  1. Remove all other Google accounts from Chrome
  2. Keep only the account you want to use with RecFix
  3. Use the extension
  4. Add back other accounts later if needed
How do I select videos for recommendations?

There are two ways to select videos:

  1. Use the "Scan" tab to automatically detect videos from your current YouTube page
  2. Use the "Manual" tab to paste YouTube video URLs directly
Where can I find my created playlist?

Your playlist will appear in:

  1. YouTube's "Library" section
  2. Under "Playlists" > "Created playlists"
  3. The playlist will be named "RecFix Recommendations" with the current date
Is my playlist private?
Yes, all playlists created by RecFix are set to private by default. Only you can see them.
Note: RecFix is continuously being improved. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please feel free to provide feedback through the Chrome Web Store.
